## 💡 About
Hero Rescuers is a side project I'm working on under my company, Kinematic Games. It's an online first responder game that supports up to 50 concurrent users.

## 👨‍💻 Tech

* Unity
* Mirror
* LazyECS
* LazyECS.Networking
* HDRP
* RTX
* NodeJS
* Jenkins
* Docker
